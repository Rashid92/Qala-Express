    A small introduction about our company

    Qala Express introduces full range of transportation and logistic service throughout Azerbaijan and the Region. It commenced its activity in 2008 and is considered to be one of the pioneers in local modern transport industry. The Company’s initial focus was development of safe and reliable transportation services in the Country. Today, Qala Express is a company specialized in both transportation and logistics.
